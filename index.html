Frequently Asked Questions

When do clocks change in the U.S.?

Clocks change twice a year. They spring forward on the second Sunday in March and fall back on the first Sunday in November. The change occurs at 2:00 AM local time.

Is it Daylight Saving or Daylight Savings?

The correct term is Daylight Saving Time (without the "s"). However, many people commonly say "Daylight Savings Time."

Why do we have Daylight Saving Time?
