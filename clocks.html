FAQs

Why 2:00 AM?

Clocks officially change at 2:00 AM local time to minimize disruption. It avoids midnight confusion and late-night business impacts.

Do all states change?

No. Hawaii and most of Arizona do not observe Daylight Saving Time. The rest of the U.S. does.
